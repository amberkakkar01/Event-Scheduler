About IEEE Computer Society


 “Start, Grow, and Advance Your Computing Career “

The IEEE Computer Society is the world’s leading membership organization dedicated to computer science and technology. Serving more than 60,000 members, the IEEE Computer Society is the trusted information, networking, and career-development source for a global community of technology leaders that includes researchers, educators, software engineers, IT professionals, employers, and students. The IEEE Computer Society sponsors more than 200 technical conferences and events each year, including the industry-oriented “Rock Stars” series, all over the world, aimed at research and industry professionals. Our publications are peer-reviewed, indexed, and authored by technology thought leaders worldwide, and include 17 scholarly journals and 13 magazines featuring the latest technology trends. We also offer cutting-edge products such as the myComputer app (available for iOS or Android), that provide unprecedented user-driven access to our content, and our Digital Library with more than 550,000 articles and papers spanning the full spectrum of computer science and technology.

About IEEE Computer Society DITU Chapter

IEEE Computer Society DITU Chapter was initiated in December 2018 under the guidance of Faculty Advisor Dr. Vishal Bharti sir. The Chapter regularly conducts events aiming to bridge the gap between academics and the industry, through technical talks and workshops. The chapter has given ample focus on developing the programming skills of members by conducting various coding competitions. Some of its recent activities include Linux Bootcamp and session on Microsoft Azure. All the sessions were done as practical ones which followed code-to-learn strategy rather than listening.
